What To Expect

Each tutorial should teach one concept well:

  • a small setup,
  • a clear learning goal,
  • runnable code, and
  • a short conclusion about what to carry forward.

For larger end-to-end tasks, see the How-To Guides. For conceptual background, see Explanations. For exact APIs, use References.
